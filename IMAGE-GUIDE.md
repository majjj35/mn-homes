## HOW TO USE:

1. **On your iPhone/computer:**
   - Rename your photos to match these exact names
   - Make sure they're .jpg format

2. **Upload to GitHub:**
   - Go to your repo → `images` folder
   - Upload all renamed photos at once
   - Commit

3. **Wait 30 seconds:**
   - Vercel auto-deploys
   - Refresh your site
   - All images appear!

---

## IMAGE TIPS:

**Hero image:**
- Wide/horizontal (not square)
- Shows a complete beautiful space
- High quality
- Not too busy (text needs to show over it)

**Portfolio & Project images:**
- Square format works best (will be cropped to square)
- Show completed projects
- Good lighting
- Professional looking

**Team photos:**
- Professional headshots or casual team photos
- Clear, well-lit
- Consistent style if multiple people
