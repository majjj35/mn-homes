About Us

Building excellence for over three decades

Over 35 Years of Excellence in Construction & Design

Founded on a commitment to quality craftsmanship and innovative design, our family business has been transforming spaces across Saudi Arabia for more than three decades. Led by Mohammed Noor Shinkar, a licensed Civil Engineer and Contractor with extensive experience in residential, commercial, and hospitality projects, we bring both technical expertise and creative vision to every project.

What began as a traditional construction company specializing in villas, apartments, hospitals, and retail developments has evolved to meet the changing needs of modern clients. Today, we combine our deep construction knowledge with contemporary interior design services, offering comprehensive solutions from ground-up builds to complete renovations and interior transformations.

Our evolution reflects our commitment to innovation while honoring the craftsmanship and attention to detail that has defined our work for 35 years. Whether you're building new, renovating existing spaces, or seeking to modernize your interiors, we bring the same dedication to excellence that has built our reputation.

Construction & Development
